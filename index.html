About DsyaClicks

Web Developer by Weekday, Photographer by Weekend.

My name is Ahdesya Harindran and I am a programmer by profession who specializes in front-end web applications, building dynamic and beautiful web pages. Photography has been my passion and hobby for at least a half a decade now, and I've been loving every minute of it. I've been exposed to different types of photography throughout the years, but some of my specialties include portrait, product and landscape photography.

I started DsyaClicks to showcase my love for photography. Check out some of the photos below to see what I've been up to.
